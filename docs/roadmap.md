# Roadmap

> Subject to change. [File a feature request](https://github.com/DyedfoxRA/wyrmflow/issues/new/choose) to vote on what matters to you.

---

## Now — this quarter (Q2 2026)

- **Auth & accounts** — sign in with GitHub, Google, GitLab, or magic link; secure keychain-backed session storage
- **Subscription billing** — Free (1 repo) and Pro (unlimited) tiers via Stripe; yearly and monthly options
- **i18n** — English, Spanish, German, Ukrainian, Polish, Simplified Chinese
- **Auto-updater** — silent background updates with signature verification; opt-out in Settings

---

## Next — Q3 2026

- **Forge integration** — GitHub PRs and CI status inline in the commit graph; GitLab MR support
- **In-app terminal** — embedded shell scoped to the current workspace
- **Submodules** — full lifecycle (add, init, update, sync, deinit) with drill-in navigation
- **Global find in files** — Cmd+Shift+F ripgrep-powered search across the working copy

---

## Later — someday

- **File history** — per-path log with blame integration
- **N-way conflict resolver** — stacked-column view for complex merge conflicts
- **Organization & team management** — shared Pro seats, invite flow
- **Notifications inbox** — in-app feed for PR reviews, CI failures, mentions
